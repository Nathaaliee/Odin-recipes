Spaghetti bolognese

Spaghetti bolognese (sometimes called spaghetti alla bolognese) is a pasta dish that is popular outside Italy, but not part of traditional Bolognese or even Italian cuisine in general. The dish is generally perceived as inauthentic when encountered by Italians abroad.

Ingredients:

  • tomatoes
  • meat
  • onion
  • spaghetti pasta
  • grated cheese
  • spices: basil, salt and pepper

Follow the steps:

  1. Boil spaghetti pasta in a boiling water with salt.
  2. Fry the meat and onion in a frying pan with hot oil.
  3. Meanwhile, make the tomato sauce, blend the tomatoes, season with basil, salt and pepper.
  4. Add the sauce to the pan when the meat is fried and boil it for a while.
  5. Pour the pasta on the plate, add the meat sauce, sprinkle with grated cheese and basil.

Bon Appetit!
