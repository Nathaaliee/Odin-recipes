Shakshouka

Shakshouka(Arabic: شكشوكة, also spelled shakshuka or chakchouka) is a Maghrebi dish, originated in Ottoman North Africa in the mid-16th century. It's an amazing dish. Let's do it and you won't regret it!

Ingredients:

  • eggs
  • olive oil
  • onion
  • garlic
  • spices: cumin, paprika and cyenne pepper
  • tomatoes for the sauce

This recipe basically has two main steps:

  1. Make the shakshuka tomato sauce. Start by sauteing chopped onions, bell peppers, and garlic with a little bit of extra virgin olive oil in a large, heavy skillet or pan (with a lid).
  2. Add the eggs and cook.
  3. Serve immediately!
